Rafting Trips

River 1

River 1 trip is our beginner/ introductiory trip into white water rafting. The rapids are smaller and the trip is shorter

River 2

River 2 trips are for those that want the larger rapids but maynot enjoy longer trips. Some rafting experience is required for river 2 trips

River 3

River 3 trips are for the thrill seeker. This longer trip has more and larger rapids that the other two trips. These trips are for the most experienced rafters and are always a good time.
